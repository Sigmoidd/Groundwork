PUBLIC UTILITY LAYER

Live map pins

Attach a point of interest by name or by street address, use the map/current location, or add a photo with EXIF GPS and let Groundwork fill the address natively. Groundwork stores the place label and address with the pin so people do not have to think in lat / long.

Pin scorecards now support accessibility details, confirmations, ratings, notes, and added photos locally in the browser.

SUBMIT A RESOURCE

Share a public or group pin

Public pins go on the open map. Group-scoped pins stay private on the relay and only appear for group members.

Search by name or address, or let a photo with EXIF GPS fill the place automatically. If no location is attached yet, Groundwork will ask for one on submit. After posting, people can confirm, rate, note, and attach more photos from each pin card.

WHAT THIS IS

              
Groundwork OKC

            

          

          

            
Groundwork dignifies the city by making it legible to everyone who lives in it.

            
The public map tracks water fountains, restrooms, outlets, shade, gardens, fishing spots, and civic resources. Group submaps let fishing crews, planting teams, cleanup routes, and trusted networks share pins without exposing them to the open map.

            
Jobs post simple neighborhood tasks. The planner generates permaculture-minded planting guides. The inbox queues relay-ready private messages. Pin scorecards now carry accessibility details, confirmations, ratings, notes, and added photos locally first.
