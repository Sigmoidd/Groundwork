WHAT THIS IS

              
Groundwork OKC

            

          

          

            
Groundwork OKC is a civic utility layer for public water, outlets, shade, gardens, and small stewardship jobs around Oklahoma City.

            
This version adds a real slippy map, a relay-ready local inbox, a stronger resource submission flow, and a germination / planting planner guided by permaculture principles like observe and interact, small and slow solutions, and stacking functions.

            
Keep the map public for clearly public or permissioned resources. Keep sensitive coordination in the inbox. Keep the real log on devices.
